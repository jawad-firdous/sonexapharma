Who We Are

Sonexa Pharma is a leading pharmaceutical distributor established in 2018, driven by a commitment to enhancing lives through access to innovative and essential healthcare solutions. With a nationwide distribution network, we specialize in life-critical domains like liver and kidney transplants, cardiology, vaccinations, diabetes, and nutrition.

We work closely with both public and private institutions, offering tailored support in the LP business, institutional supply chains, and medical professional outreach.
