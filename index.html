About Us

Founded in 2018, Sonexa Pharma is a trusted name in the pharmaceutical distribution sector, renowned for delivering exceptional quality and service. With a specialized focus in liver and kidney transplants, cardiology, vaccination, diabetes, nutrition, antibiotics, and nutraceuticals, we empower healthcare providers to offer life-saving treatments across the region.

Our operations are built upon a deep commitment to healthcare excellence, supported by a vast distribution network that ensures timely and efficient delivery. At Sonexa Pharma, we combine innovation, transparency, and passion to improve patient outcomes and elevate the healthcare industry in Pakistan and beyond.

Today, we stand as a strategic partner to many hospitals, institutions, and health organizations, championing quality healthcare solutions with unwavering integrity and professionalism.
